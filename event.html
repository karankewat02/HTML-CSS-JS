Past Events

Indigo Triwizard Creative Writing Contest

The "Indigo Triwizard Creative Writing Contest" organized online by Team Indigo GGITS under two subcategories - Story Writing and Article Writing was the first event under a non-technical theme and artistic subject. With the participation of 100+ students from GGITS and GGCT 1st year all branches, the contest was conducted smoothly using Google Forms. A week was provided for submission of same and top 10 winners in both categories were announced. Also, winners were provided with achievement certificates and prizes were distributed.



International Women's Day Event

The "International Women's Day Event " major offline event organised in collaboration with Girl Up Jabalpur on 8th March 2021 was a success with 200 attendees. Main focus was the Youth Welfare and Women Empowerment perfectly presented by Arni Khare and Nandini Tandon, co-founders and co- presidents of Girl Up Jabalpur and amiably assisted by Anuj Bhatt and Abhishek Shrivastava ( members of group ). The whole presentation made people aware about the mission, vision and goals of the club.



Pie and AI event

The "Pie and AI event" allied and approved by Deeplearning.AI, a minor online event joined by more than 100 students (including 20-25 from other colleges) organised by Team Indigo GGITS on 20th September, 2020 was well conducted on platform - Google Meets. The speaker was Mr. Muditya Raghav and the theme of this informative session was - "Breaking into AI" specifically, Introduction and Information about Trending Technologies, AI and ML. The student ambassadorship was applied for the same and we are now Deeplearning.AI ambassadors for Jabalpur city.
